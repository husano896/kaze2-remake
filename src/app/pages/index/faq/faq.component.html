FAQ

[IOS] 為何我的遊戲在接耳機時才有聲音？ / Why my game only have sounds when headphones are connected? iPhone / iPad的靜音模式會使遊戲無聲音，請關閉後才能從喇叭發出聲音。
iPhone / iPad has silent mode, please turn off to play sounds from the speaker.

[Account] 我可以使用舊版 (ゲームの缶詰) 存檔嗎？ / Can I login with old version (game-can) account? 請前往此處 (Please follow this link)
請注意於存檔搬移後，新版本的存檔並不會回存至舊版，同時相同存檔可以搬移不只一次，但無法將帳號搬移到現有的ID.
Please be careful that saves in new version won't be synced to old version, also you can migrate the save file for more than one time, But you can't overwrite pre-existed IDs.
